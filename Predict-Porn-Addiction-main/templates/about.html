About Us

Our team has developed a specialized AI prediction website using Flask, aimed at assessing and predicting Porn Addiction based on user-provided data. We understand the sensitive nature of this issue and are committed to offering a discreet and non-judgmental platform to assist individuals in understanding and addressing potential concerns related to porn consumption.

Our Mission

At the core of our mission is the belief that technology can be harnessed for positive change. By leveraging artificial intelligence, we aim to provide a tool that not only predicts the likelihood of porn addiction but also serves as a starting point for individuals to seek support and make informed decisions about their well-being.

How it Works

Our AI prediction model analyzes the data you provide to generate insights into your potential vulnerability to porn addiction. The process is designed to be secure, confidential, and focused on providing valuable information without compromising your privacy.
